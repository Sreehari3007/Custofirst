About Us

Custofirst Enterprises Pvt Ltd is based in Kasaragod, Kerala. We specialize in rice-based products like Puttu podi and Idli rice, relabeled under our own brand or white-labeled for distributors in the UK and beyond. Our mission is to offer premium quality at competitive prices.
